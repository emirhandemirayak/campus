What Problem Are We Solving?

🎯

Real Problems

Fragmented Platform Issue: Students use different platforms for books (Sahibinden), roommates (Instagram), and events (Facebook). This fragmentation leads to wasted time and security risks.

Security Concerns: Open platforms lack identity verification, leading to high fraud risk.

Information Overload: Lack of reliable information sources about the university.

✨

Campus App Solution

Single Platform: All needs in one app - book exchange, roommate finding, event discovery.

Secure Environment: Membership only with .edu.tr email, identity verification system.

Smart Recommendations: AI-powered matching and personalized content.
